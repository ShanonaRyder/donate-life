What We Do

We save lives and improve health through the promotion of organ, eye and tissue donation. Through our various outreach programs, we reach thousands of people throughout the Pacific Northwest every year.

Together with our community, Donate Life Northwest has registered more than 3 million Oregonians to be organ, eye and tissue donors. Join us in giving hope to those who need it most. Register today!
